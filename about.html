About

User Experience Design - User Interaction Design - Media Arts - Design Consulting - Product Design - Type Design - Graphics - Architecture

These are the wide range of areas I have been working over the past few years as a creative practitioner. After graduating as an architect back in 2009, I started a design practice with three friends and a (cool) professor to work on multi-disciplinary design projects. This got me excited about the holistic approach of design, which can be applied to all domains. I went ahead to apply for Masters in Design at Harvard Graduate School of Design where I found myself digging deeper into the ideas of user experience.
