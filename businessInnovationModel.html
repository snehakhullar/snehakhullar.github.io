Business Innovation Model

Market Research - Brainstorming - Whiteboarding - Innovation Model - Business Plan

The project involved designing a business innovation model for the specific scope defined by the partner clients. The design process dealt with doing industry research to understand and redefine the problem. Several alternatives were discussed to narrow down to a final solution, which proposed inducing transparency in the product value chain (which according to the study was the underlying problem).

Project in collaboration with Michael Quinn and Jielu Lu at Harvard University
