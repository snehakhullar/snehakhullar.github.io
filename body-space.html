Body-Space

Experience Design - Spatial Experiment

Departing from an architectural perception of space, the project interrogates human relationship with the space we consume everyday. The performance is a forgotten interpretation of one particular space - entrance vestibule. Idea of entrance or privilege to enter somewhere is completely denied by everyday use of this highly controlled framework. The idea is to create a dense filter made of human bodies inside this volume and initiate four different scenarios emerging from interactions with passerby.

Project in collaboration with Nikola Bojic at Harvard University
