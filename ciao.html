CIAO

Product Concept - Research - Idea Development - Prototyping

The project concept was based on addressing wasteful energy consumption in a work-environment. An application was perceived to induce behavior change to promote efficient energy use. A research of products that have led to green behavior was carried out and the finding was that visualization of energy performance could lead to positive change. Hence a platform for visualizing energy performance in a work-space was designed. The model allowed the user to select the real-time data input from building management system, visualization semantics and location of display (personal or public devices or systems).

Project in collaboration with Hung Fai Tang and Jennifer Hepworth at MIT
