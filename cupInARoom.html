Cup in a room

Concept and Layout Design - End-to-end Implementation

The JavaScript application explores browser based 3D modelling. The project defines a cause-effect relationship between the cup and the room. The location of the cup determines the design of the possible seating or approach to reach the cup. The sliders are the application interaction contols governing the zoom, effects and x,y,z position of the cup.

Try the application (WebGL supported browser required)
