Note-ing

Concept Design - User Research - Persona Creation - Wireframing

The project at MIT Mobile Experinece Lab involed developing a solution for improving a public launch or presentation experience. Several ideas from augmented reality audience-speaker feedback to haptic gestures were explored. The final iteration of the product was an application for note-taking and sharing information at an event. The design took into consideration discussion opportunities though public channels like Twitter. It even provided an offline notes package for audience to share and take back after the event.

Project in collaboration with Nicholas Wallen, Leonardo Giusti, Zoe Schladow and Amar Boghani at MIT Mobile Experience Lab
