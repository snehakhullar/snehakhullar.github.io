Harvard Course Networks

Concept Design - Data Processing - Wireframing - Visual Design - Front-end implementation

Harvard University offers more than eight thousand courses to students across more than a hundred departments. To understand this diversity in pedagogy we decided to visualize the relationship between different academic concentrations at Harvard. Beyond building a neat visualization, we intended to design a tool which would help Harvard students select courses.
